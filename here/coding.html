WHY CODING ?

There’s been a lot of buzz about young people learning to code lately. This year, almost 200 million students around the world were exposed to coding through the Hour of Code event. And over 90% of moroccan parents want programming added to their child’s curriculum.¹ In today’s digital world, coding is a fundamental skill alongside math and reading, but too few students have the opportunity to learn to program because it is rarely taught in school. Opportunity catcher's mission is to create a fun and engaging platform where people, not only find opportunities to learn coding, but also to interact with mutiple talented and experimented mentors on a local, national and international level.
